Spectacular White Water Rafting.

Family Run Since 1982

We have close to 40 years of guide experience taking travelers through the spectacular Idaho scenery of the Salmon River. Our Salmon River runs can offer a respite from your hectic world, free of distractions, or offer a well needed adrenaline rush. Our experienced guides work closley with our individual clients to customize a rafting adventure that will be an unforgettable and unparralled white water journey. We can offer a choice of relaxing, exhilerating, individual or group adventures. You customize your vacation and we'll make it happen.
