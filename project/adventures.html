Our Adventures

Scenic Adventure

This adventure offers true relaxation along with superior wildlife photographic opportunities. While floating down the middle part of the Salmon River you’ll have an opportunity to view various types of wildlife abundant in the area. Through binoculars or your camera lens you’ll have an opportunity to spot eagles, big horn sheep, elk, and black bear. We have many stops on the way where you’ll have an opportunity to visit hot springs, old mines, and various caves.

This adventure is geared for all skill levels including novice rafters, wildlife, and nature enthusiasts. The adventure accommodates all levels of photographers and nature watchers. Our guides are experienced in identifying picturesque opportunities of wildlife and that of our scenic beauty.

Cost start at $75 per person providing a four hour float Through the National Wilderness Area. The cost includes parking and transportation to the various drop points.

Rapids Adventure

The Salmon River offers diverse river adventures. Including the opportunity to fulfill your need for the adrenaline rush of rapids. The level of adventure you choose on the Salmon River is open to your imagination and appetite. The Salmon River provides white water rafting suitable for intermediate to advanced skill levels, but if you’re seeking other types of adventure the Salmon River also offers kayaking that allows you to navigate rapids individually, challenging a more advanced skill set. Our rafts are geared for four or more people and are guide controlled.

The cost is dependent upon the adventure you choose and determined by your skill level. A half day white water rafting adventure starts at $75 per person, a self-guided kayak rafting adventure is $150 per day. We also offer two – five day trips, prices dependent on skill level and adventure you choose.

Vacation Adventure

The lower Salmon River provides opportunities for camping, paddle boarding, kayaking, and swimming on pure white sand beaches giving your family and friends an unforgettable vacation. Let us plan a five – seven day adventure for your friends and family. Our service will provide you with camping permits, fishing gear, paddle boats, rafting and kayaking adventures. This part of the river in June and July offers calmer water, quiet pools, gentler rapids, sand bars, and beaches. Fishing in the river includes rainbow trout, small mouth bass, steelhead, and salmon.

Camping Permits start at $25 per day, fishing license for non-residents $55 for three days, we also provide residents fishing license at the state cost. We offer rentals starting at $35 per day. Day rafting trips start at a cost of $75 per person. We have all inclusive prices for our vacations, please contact us and we will provide you with all the necessary information.
