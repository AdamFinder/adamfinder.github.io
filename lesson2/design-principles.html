Law of Proximity

Wowhead

wowhead.com

Wowhead depicts the law of proximity well. All the subjects are clearly separated by their related subject and are in themselves links to more in depth information regarding the topic. Everything is close together and easily readable.

White space and clean Design

Icy-Veins

Icy-Veins.com

Icy-Veins does a good job at using white space to help separate and neatly organize information on the web page. Clear breaks in the information help the reader understand when a new topic is starting.

Hick's Law

Best Buy

Bestbuy.com

Best Buy's website helps show the Hick's Law principle. It makes it's filters and item locations on their site easly understandable and accessable with many different filter combinations to help narrow searches.
