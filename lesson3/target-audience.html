Target Audience

Ideal Audience

Personas

  1. Roger Ramjet is a pilot for tourists around northern Arizona.Roger relies heavily on weather sites in order to plan his flight patterns and excursions for his customers.
  2. Jeffery is a ground crew lead at a midsized airport. Reviewing the site helps him predict scheduling he may need for his employees based on future and current weather patterns.

Scenarios

  1. Roger's business depends on good weather for air travel. He has three tours planned for tomorrow and uses the website to determine the weather over Sedona and the Grand Canyon to see which desinations will be most accomodating for his customers.
  2. Ground crews, and flight organizers review the site daily for upcoming weather changes that may have an impact on scheduling crews, delaying or grounding flights. The information received from the site will be utilized to communicate up and down the chain at small and large airports to help maintain schedules.
  3. Roger needs to travel from Arizona to Houston Texas. He heard that a huge storm is settling over Texas. Roger consults the website to determine best flight route.
  4. Jeffery runs a ground crew at a midsized airport. He schedules crews to help keep flight schedules. He reviews the site several times a day to determine upcoming needs. Although the temperature and overall weather is currently acceptable for flights to take off and land a large cold front is approaching predicting a large drop in temperature with moisture. This will allow jeffery to call in extra staff that can help clear runways if needed and de-ice planes for take off.
  5. Roger's friend Jerry needs a favor. Jerry is a farmer and asks Roger to help him dust his fields with pesticide. Roger checks the weather report to ideentify the best days to help his friend.
